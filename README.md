# Come Home

A 2D horror dungeon crawler made in Unity 6 for WVU Game Design, Project 3.

## Story
You are a detective called to investigate a home where a woman hasn't been seen in years. Once you enter, the doors lock behind you. The house is a  generated maze of rooms with each one revealing more about what happened here through collectible lore notes.

The deeper you go, the more you realize. The daughter was never dead and she's been here the whole time.

## How to Play
- **WASD** — move
- **Walk into lore objects** — read notes
- **J** — open journal to review collected notes
- **Find the daughter** (yellow circle) before reaching the exit
- **Reach the green exit marker** to escape
- If the Mother catches you and drains your HP — game over

## Two Endings
- **Good ending** — escape with the daughter
- **Bad ending** — mother kills you

## Features
- Procedurally generated connected dungeon rooms
- Mother NPC AI that chases and damages the player
- Lore note collection system with journal
- Health bar UI
- Two scene outcomes (WinScene / GameOver)
